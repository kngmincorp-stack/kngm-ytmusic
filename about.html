About
YouTube Music Player

A desktop music player built for people who just want to listen.

Our Mission

We believe music should be lightweight and accessible.

Listening to music shouldn’t require a full web browser, gigabytes of RAM, or constant interruptions from ads. YouTube Music Player was built with a simple idea: take the music you already love on YouTube and deliver it through the fastest, most focused player possible.

No new accounts, no subscriptions, no bloated software. Just your existing YouTube library, played as pure audio, wrapped in a clean dark interface.
