Everything You Need to Listen

⚡

Audio-Only Playback

No video overhead, no wasted bandwidth. YouTube Music Player streams pure audio via ffplay, using a fraction of the resources compared to a browser. Your CPU and battery will thank you.

❤

Your Liked Videos

Sign in with your YouTube account and your entire Liked Videos library appears instantly. No importing, no syncing delays & just your music, ready to play.

🔍

YouTube Music Search

Search the entire YouTube Music catalog directly from the app. Results are filtered to songs only & no podcasts, no videos, no noise. Find exactly what you want to hear.

📋

Custom Queue

Build playlists on the fly from your Liked Videos or search results. Your queue persists between sessions, so you never lose your carefully curated lineup.

🎧

Listen Together

Host a listening session and invite friends on the same network. Everyone hears the same track at the same time with real-time synchronization. Perfect for shared workspaces and hangouts.

🔀

Shuffle & Repeat

All the playback modes you expect: shuffle, repeat all, repeat one, or sequential play. Control everything with keyboard shortcuts for a seamless listening experience.

🌐

Multi-Language Support

Available in 8 languages: English, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese. The app auto-detects your system language on first launch.

🌑

Dark Theme

A Spotify-inspired dark interface that’s easy on the eyes during late-night listening sessions. Designed for focus, built for comfort.

System Requirements
