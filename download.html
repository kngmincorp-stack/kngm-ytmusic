Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes, completely free. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no paywalls. All features are available to everyone.

Is it safe to use?

Yes. The application does not collect personal data beyond what is needed for YouTube authentication. Your credentials are handled entirely by Google’s official sign-in flow through Chrome. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I sign in?

Click the “Sign In” button in the app. Google Chrome will open automatically for you to authenticate with your YouTube/Google account. Once signed in, your liked videos will sync immediately.

Does it work on Mac or Linux?

Currently, YouTube Music Player is available for Windows only. Mac and Linux versions are not available at this time.

Do I need to install anything else?

You need Google Chrome installed for the sign-in process. The app bundles ffplay for audio playback, so no additional software is required.
